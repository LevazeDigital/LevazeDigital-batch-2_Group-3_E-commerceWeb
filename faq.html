Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a purchase?

You can make a purchase in person at the book hub website. We are no longer a self-service Bookstore, so you will need to check out at the register with a staff volunteer.

How do I know how much to pay for the books?

All merchandise is priced and books are sold by the price on the back. We collect sales tax at the time of purchase.

So, why should I buy books at the bookhub?

There are several reasons why you should make your purchases of Dharma books and other items in the bookhub.

Can I special order a book?

Yes, you can.

Do you need any help running the bookhub? Are there volunteer opportunities?

Absolutely! Feel free to contact us regarding this.
